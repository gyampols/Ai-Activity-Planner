Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

1. Introduction

AI Activity Planner ("we", "our", "the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address: For account identification and communication
  • Username: For account identification
  • Password: Stored securely using hashing (Werkzeug)
  • Profile information: Name, age, gender, height, weight, timezone (optional)

2.2 OAuth Authentication Data

When you connect third-party services, we collect:

  • Google OAuth: Google ID, email, access tokens, refresh tokens for Google Calendar and Google Fit data
  • Fitbit OAuth: Fitbit user ID, access tokens, refresh tokens for activity and sleep data
  • Oura: Access tokens for sleep and activity data

2.3 Activity and Appointment Data

We store information you create:

  • Custom activities (name, duration, intensity, location, dependencies)
  • Appointments (title, date, time, type)
  • Generated weekly plans

2.4 Location Data

With your permission, we collect:

  • City or location for weather forecasts (Open-Meteo API)
  • This data is not stored; only used for real-time weather information

2.5 Biometric and Fitness Data

When you connect fitness trackers, we access:

  • Recent sleep data (duration, quality)
  • Activity data (steps, active minutes)
  • Heart rate data (from Google Fit, Fitbit, or Oura)
  • This data is accessed via API and not permanently stored

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service: Generate personalized activity plans based on your data
  • Improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Communication: Send important updates about your account or the Service
  • Integration Services: Export plans to Google Calendar, fetch fitness data
  • AI Processing: Send anonymized data to OpenAI GPT-4 for plan generation

4. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

4.1 Google Services

Data Accessed: Email, profile, Google Calendar events, Google Fit data

Purpose: Calendar export, fitness data retrieval, authentication

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Fitbit

Data Accessed: Sleep data, activity data

Purpose: Personalize activity recommendations

Fitbit's Privacy Policy: Fitbit Privacy

4.3 Oura

Data Accessed: Sleep data, readiness scores

Purpose: Optimize activity planning based on recovery

Oura's Privacy Policy: Oura Privacy

4.4 OpenAI

Data Sent: Anonymized activity preferences, weather data, fitness summary (no personal identifiers)

Purpose: AI-powered weekly plan generation using GPT-4

OpenAI's Privacy Policy: OpenAI Privacy

4.5 Open-Meteo

Data Sent: Location coordinates for weather forecast

Purpose: Weather-based activity planning

Note: Open-Meteo is a free API; data is not stored by them

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage Location

  • Database: SQLite (or Cloud SQL when configured)
  • Hosting: Google Cloud Run (us-central1)
  • OAuth Tokens: Stored encrypted in database

5.2 Security Measures

  • Passwords are hashed using Werkzeug's security functions
  • OAuth tokens are stored securely and never exposed to frontend
  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Session-based authentication with secure cookies

5.3 Data Retention

  • Account data is retained until you delete your account
  • OAuth tokens are retained until you disconnect the service
  • Fitness data from APIs is not permanently stored

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your profile information in Settings.

6.2 Disconnect Services

You can disconnect any OAuth service at any time, which revokes our access to that service's data.

6.3 Delete Account

You can delete your account from the Settings page. This will:

  • Permanently delete all your activities and appointments
  • Revoke all OAuth connections
  • Remove your account information from our database

6.4 Data Export

You can export your activity plans to Google Calendar at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Google OAuth Compliance

AI Activity Planner's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Limited Use Disclosure:

  • We only request the minimum necessary scopes (email, profile, calendar.events, fitness data)
  • Data is used solely to provide the Service's features
  • We do not sell Google user data to third parties
  • Human reading of data is limited to debugging and support purposes only

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use session cookies for:

  • Maintaining user login sessions
  • OAuth state verification

We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification for material changes

11. International Users

The Service is hosted in the United States (Google Cloud us-central1). By using the Service, you consent to the transfer of your data to the United States.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through our support channels.
