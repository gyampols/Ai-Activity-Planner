About AI Activity Planner

Our Mission

AI Activity Planner helps you maintain a balanced and active lifestyle by intelligently scheduling your favorite activities throughout the week. Whether you're into running, yoga, swimming, or any other activity, our AI-powered system creates personalized plans that consider your preferences, fitness levels, and schedule constraints.

How It Works

1. Log Your Activities

Add activities you enjoy with details like location, duration, intensity level, and any dependencies (weather, equipment, etc.). The more information you provide, the better your personalized plan will be.

2. Connect Your Devices (Optional)

Integrate your Fitbit or Oura account to help the AI understand your activity patterns and recovery needs. This allows for more accurate and personalized planning.

3. Generate Your Weekly Plan

Our AI assistant, powered by OpenAI's ChatGPT, analyzes your activities and creates a balanced weekly schedule. The plan considers intensity distribution, recovery time, weather dependencies, and location requirements to ensure optimal results.

Technology

This application is built with Flask and deployed on Google App Engine. It uses OpenAI's ChatGPT API for intelligent activity planning and supports integration with popular fitness tracking devices.
